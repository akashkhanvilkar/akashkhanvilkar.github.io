    Customer Churn Analysis

    Customer churn means a customer ending their relationship with a company for any reason. In this project a dataset of a telephone company was taken and key aspects of its customers who churned were analysed. The goal was to find the qualities of churners and devise plans and schemes for the company to retain majority of them. Hence, improving profits.

    Salary Predictor

    This salary predictor gives you your approximate annual package for a salesforce(CRM) related job based upon 4 parameters (total experience, job skills, job title and location). Data regarding available salesforce jobs was collected from glassdoor and scrapped using selenium and beautiful soup package of Python. Data cleaning and model building done using pandas and scikit learn.
